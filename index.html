Why Pick & Drop

This is a mobile platform/ application that enables a shuttle driver to pick schoolchildren from their homes and take them to school as well as return them home. This application indicates the geographical location of each child on the driver’s smartphone screen showing which child is going/not going to school. The parent is notified every time the child boards and disembarks the school bus/shuttle.

Real-Time Communication

This application provides real-time communication between the parent and the school where the child studies. Parents do not need to worry about the safety of their children while travelling to school and back home every day

Safety and security

With real-time monitoring capability, a parent can monitor the bus’s location with the child aboard, the driver will accurately locate the destination of the child thus eliminating child theft/loss/trafficking.

Convenience and flexibility

A parent can request shuttle service to pick the child incase he/she is unable to personally take the child to school/ bring back home

Timely delivery

Delays caused by children not going to school, child not being ready by pickup time among others are minimized, as the driver will only pick children who are ready and going to school on that day. Children will not miss classes by being late.
