Can you take your child to school everyday?

Many parents would opt to take their children to school and bring them back home themselves. This would the children reach school at the required and the children would be secure and healthy at their own parents hands.

But many of these parents do not have family cars to transport their children to school everyday, but they would want to send their children to good school which are a little bit far from home.

Though some of the parents have cars, but due too much commitment at work or at times when they are not at home to take children to school, it is still impossible for them to transport their kids to school.

Should I transport MY child to school myself, but I do not have a car. Should I use BODABODAS, but they are so risky for young children and it is too early in the morning that this children are supposed to report for classes, this is too cold on a BODABODA for their health.

Many parents opt for shuttle services because their family does not own a family car to drive their children to school. They also have the mentality that Bodabodas are risky and dangerous in terms of accidents and the exposure to cold air that can easily cause health complications “Ssali says”. Despite the convenience associated with using shuttle services, some parents note that their children’s physical and emotional needs are not catered for during the journeys.

And the shuttle services are not secure enough, the children are just mixed up in the taxi, so the drivers do not even have any idea on where to leave or pick a particular child, this takes them alot of time to ensure all the children they are supposed to pick are in the car. The children reach school late.

What should I do?
